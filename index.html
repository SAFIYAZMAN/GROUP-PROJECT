Our Cafe

Kumpulan Kopi Mesin Sdn. Bhd. is a bumiputera status company aim to deliver that perfect cup of coffee along with delicious specialty drink such as German Iced Coffee, Cappuccinos, Iced Coffees and Frappe. We also offer western food and desserts. All of our food use only the finest ingredients and utilizing traditional Malay, and Western cooking methods, providing an excellent taste for all type age of customers. Kopi Mesin was started out as a small coffee shop at Kubang Kerian, Kelantan in 2016 and has now become one of the major coffee shop of its kind in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. All our branches have provide a bold presence for local perspective.

We are passionate about the quality of our products and the standards of our service. We love what we do and we want our customers to feel part of this experience. We understand the expectation from our customers and we aim to deliver the experience through the right balance of sights, smells, tastes and sounds which time and again appeal to their way of life a personal experience each time they visit one of our branches.
